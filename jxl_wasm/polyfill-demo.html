What is this?

This polyfill automatically detects and converts JXL images to PNG in the browser. It works by:

  • Scanning for <img src="*.jxl"> tags
  • Watching for dynamically added images (MutationObserver)
  • Optionally patching new Image() constructor
  • Caching converted images for performance

Usage

<!-- Just include the polyfill -->
<script type="module" src="polyfill.js"></script>

<!-- Use JXL images normally -->
<img src="image.jxl" alt="My image">
